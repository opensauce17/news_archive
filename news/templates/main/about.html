The World News Archive is a news aggregator with an archiving feature. It is part of a portfolio suite featured on mikehyland.com

The application gathers, archives and then displays news titles published by world wide news outlets from six countries within seven categories. News data has been gathered since January of 2020 and archived titles can be searched by date and per category under the country menu bar on the left.

The World News Archive is currently a non-commercial service and falls under fair use. The World News Archive claims no rights to any data, titles or images that it may feature.

This site is optimised for mobile dimensions from 375px and higher and the chrome browser on any device
