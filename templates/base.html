About The South African News Archive

The South African News Archive is part of a portfolio suite featured on mikehyland.com

This app utilises the News API to gather and display news articles published by South African outlets.

A python script runs every 30 minutes to gather news article details and stores them in a mysql database. The web application displays the news per selected category and date. The navigation items will display today's news in the selected categories.

The search mechanism query's the database by date and category and allows the user to view archived news articles. All news articles are diplayed by latest to oldest.
